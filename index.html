Astra – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Thank you for using Astra (“we”, “our”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how Astra collects, uses, and protects your information.

By using Astra, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User Account Information

  • Email address
  • Password (handled securely by Firebase Authentication)

Your password is not stored by Astra. It is managed by Firebase Authentication.

1.2 Local App Data (Stored Only on Your Device)

  • Spending records that you manually enter in the Spend Analyzer
  • Goals that you create in the Goals page
  • Inner Peace content, such as:
    • Selected Sai Baba picture & message of the day
    • Any related data shown inside the app

This data is stored locally on your phone and is not uploaded to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your user account via Firebase Authentication
  • Display your spends, goals, and inner peace content inside the app
  • Allow you to share content via your device's share feature

We do NOT upload spends/goals to our servers, read SMS/Gmail/contacts/call logs, collect financial data from other apps, sell or share your data with advertisers or other companies.

3. Data Storage and Security

Firebase Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication only for login/account management (email & password). Provided by Google, it follows industry-standard security and compliance.

Local SQLite Database

Your spending data and goals stay on your device. If you uninstall Astra, this local data is deleted.

4. Sharing of Information

Astra does not share your personal information with third-parties. Content only leaves your device when you choose to share (e.g., WhatsApp, email, etc.) using your device's features—not our servers.

5. Permissions We Use

  • INTERNET – For login and Firebase communication.
  • VIBRATE – For optional haptic feedback.
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS – For audio playback in Inner Peace.

We do NOT request: SMS, notification access, background location, call logs, contacts, camera, microphone, or external storage read/write permissions.

6. Children’s Privacy

Astra is not targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal info from children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child has provided data, and we will act appropriately.

7. Your Choices and Controls

  • Log out anytime
  • Uninstall app to remove local data
  • Request Firebase account deletion by contacting us

Contact: astracreativelabs@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. “Last updated” date will be changed accordingly. Please review periodically.

9. Contact Us

For questions or requests: astracreativelabs@gmail.com
